Welcome to my world

Where magic meets the inanimate, I bring life.

In the year 1991, among the misty mountains of Northern Thailand, a girl named Supalak was born. Growing up as an only child in the warm embrace of her single mother, she discovered a world that others often missed. From the moment she could first hold a pencil, she was enchanted by the art of drawing. In the quiet solitude of her childhood, she mastered the art of observation, imagining a world where every still object could breathe and move, secretly practicing the magic of bringing the inanimate to life.

This lifelong passion led her to Mae Fah Luang university with Bachelor of Science Program in Multimedia Technology and Animation, turning her natural curiosity into professional mastery. Her journey soon took her into the fast-paced world of advertising. In 2014, her talent was recognized on a global stage when she was entrusted with two major projects that were shortlisted at the Cannes Lions. It was a defining moment, proving that the silent dreams of a girl from the mountains could capture the heart of the world.

Now, She continues to push the boundaries of visual storytelling as a Motion Graphic and Animation Specialist. While she remains a respected voice in the advertising industry, she is now seeking new horizons to explore. With a heart rooted in creativity and a mind fueled by technology, she is ready to bring her unique "magic" to the tech, gaming, animation, or film industries. She is looking for a team that values innovation and the art of making the world move, one frame at a time.
